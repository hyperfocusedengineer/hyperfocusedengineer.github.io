The Problem

The agentic AI era needs an application layer for everyone.

The world is investing $185B+ in AI infrastructure optimized for agentic workloads — systems capable of autonomous reasoning and complex task execution. But today's agent tools (Manus, AutoGPT, CrewAI) are still built for developers. They require workflow configuration, integration wiring, and prompt engineering. And the interface is always the same generic chatbot.

Gumball is the missing layer: it translates the power of agentic AI into custom workspaces that anyone can create, run, and refine — without writing a single line of code.

What Makes Gumball Different

Not a chatbot — the UI itself is the agent

Most AI tools give you a chat window. Gumball gives you an agentic workspace — Claude autonomously analyzes your task, reasons about what you need, and builds a custom interface with the right mix of charts, data, drafts, and insights. A price tracker gets market signals. A content agent gets drafts and tone controls. A research agent gets structured sources.

The multi-turn conversational refinement is itself an agentic workflow — the AI reasons, proposes, and adapts based on your feedback. The workspace is the agent.

How It Works

Three steps. No setup required.

💬
1. Have a conversation
Tell Gumball what you want your agent to do. It asks smart follow-up questions to understand your needs, then builds your agent.
🎨
2. Get a custom workspace
Your agent comes with a workspace designed specifically for its task — the right mix of charts, data, drafts, and insights.
🔄
3. Run it anytime
Trigger your agent manually or set it on a schedule. Each run delivers fresh, real results to your workspace.
✏️
4. Refine naturally
Want to change something? Just tell your agent. Every edit creates a new version — you can always go back.
