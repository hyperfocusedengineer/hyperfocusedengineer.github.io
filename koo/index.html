The Story

Why this exists

Parenting data is scattered everywhere — feeding logs in one app, sleep schedules in another, growth measurements on paper, photos buried in camera rolls. When multiple caregivers are involved (grandparents, nannies, co-parents), nobody has the full picture.

Koo is a single hub for everything. Log activities by voice or tap, track growth against WHO percentile curves (0–24 months, gender-specific), and share milestones through a private Village feed visible only to invited family. Each caregiver gets role-based access — admins manage everything, caretakers log activities, family members see milestones, consultants get read-only data views.

It works offline first. Activities are cached locally and sync via Supabase Realtime when connected. Grounded in WHO, AAP, and CDC guidelines — not internet parenting advice.

Features

🎤

Voice Logging

Say "4 oz bottle" and the form auto-fills. Natural speech recognition transforms words into structured activity data.

📈

WHO Growth Charts

Clinical-grade percentile tracking (3rd–97th) for weight and length, 0–24 months, gender-specific.

🏠

Village Feed

Share milestones privately with invited family. Reactions, celebrations, and confetti — no public exposure.

👥

Collaborative Care

Invite caregivers with role-based access. Admin, caretaker, family, and consultant roles with different permissions.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Feeding, sleep, and diaper trends with day/week/month views, comparative charts, and trend indicators.

☁️

Offline-First Sync

Log activities without internet. Changes queue locally and sync via Supabase Realtime when connected.

💤

Sleep Timer

Live countdown for nap and bedtime tracking with haptic feedback and duration logging.

🤖

AI Chat (Coming Soon)

Age-aware parenting Q&A powered by Google Gemini, grounded in WHO, CDC, and AAP guidelines.
