Why Zeroshot?

After years of building production systems at scale, I realized I was consuming AI as a black box. APIs, SDKs, pre-trained models — powerful, but I didn't understand what was happening underneath.

Zeroshot is my commitment to learning from zero. Refreshing Python fundamentals, implementing ML algorithms without frameworks, understanding the math behind gradient descent, backpropagation, and attention mechanisms. Each concept is validated through hands-on experiments, not just theory.

The name says it all — starting with zero assumptions, building real intuition one experiment at a time.
