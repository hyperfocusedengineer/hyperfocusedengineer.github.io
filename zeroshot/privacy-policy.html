ZEROSHOT AI

Privacy Policy

Introduction

ZeroShot AI ("we", "our", or "us") is an educational application that teaches Python programming, machine learning, and artificial intelligence through interactive coding missions. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use ZeroShot AI.

Information We Collect

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. Account creation is optional — you can use the app as a guest without providing any personal information.

Progress Data: Your learning progress, including completed levels, star ratings, XP, and in-progress code. This data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

Preferences: Your app settings such as difficulty mode and onboarding completion status, stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your learning progress
  • To personalize the learning experience based on your difficulty preferences
  • To track which levels and tracks you've completed

Data Storage and Security

ZeroShot AI follows a local-first approach. All your progress data, preferences, and saved code are stored directly on your device using browser localStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly create an account for cloud sync (coming soon).

Python code you write is executed entirely within your browser using Skulpt (an in-browser Python interpreter). Your code is never sent to any server for execution.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

ZeroShot AI is an educational app suitable for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app can be used fully in guest mode without providing any personal information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through account creation, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the app without creating an account (guest mode)
  • Access all data stored about you (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete all your data by clearing the app's localStorage or using the reset progress option in your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device for as long as you use the app. Clearing your browser data or using the in-app reset option will permanently remove all locally stored data. If you create an account, server-side data will be deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy and updating the "Last Updated" date below.
