Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

TossUp is a cricket auction and club management platform built by Hyperfocused Engineer. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

Data We Collect

When you create an account:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account identification.
  • Display name — shown to other users in clubs and auctions.
  • Club and auction data — clubs you create, auctions you host, bids you place, and event RSVPs.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and manage sessions.
  • To enable real-time auction participation and club management.
  • To display public club and tournament information on the explore gateway.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and real-time subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — hosting and deployment. Privacy Policy

We do not use advertising networks or analytics trackers.

Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security (RLS) policies.
  • All communication uses HTTPS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are managed by Supabase Auth with JWT sessions.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. Account deletion removes all associated data permanently.

Your Rights

  • Request data deletion by contacting us.
  • Browse public content without creating an account.
